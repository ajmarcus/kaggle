"In the memoir, ""Narciso Rodriguez"" by Narciso Rodriguez there were many moods created such as, upbeat, happy, and exciting. The first mood that the memoir showed was upbeat. ""Passionate Cuban music filled the air, mixing with the aromas of the kitchen."" This was very upbeat and welcoming tune to listen to in the kitchen rather than talking or silence. The second mood displayed in this memoir was happiness. ""All of these cultures came together in a great solidarity and friendship."" This is a happy thought and time when different cultures can come together to have friendship. The last mood that the memoir portrays is excitment. Here, the innocence of childhood, the congregation of family and friends, and endless celebrations."" This tells us that there are celebrations, and faimly and friends coming together, which is a very exciting time. In conclusion family and friends can make all the difference in a persons life, just as it did for Narciso Rodriguez."
