"In the essay Rough Road Ahead: Do Not Exceed Posted Speed Limit, the setting affects the story very much for many reasons. First, it is in the summer, so it is very hot. Also, it is in the desert in the west, so there is a very warm climate already. Third, there is not any civilization on the shortcut he follows, so there is no way for the author to get any water to replenish his body with. This is how the features of the setting affect the cyclist in the story."
